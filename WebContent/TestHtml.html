Murder in Donegal

Police arrived on the scene in a holiday home in Donegal to find a gruesome murder had taken place. The victim is Matt, a young man in his late 20's and the co-owner of "SquishR", a revolutionary file compression system set to make him and his partner millions. There are four suspects who were with the victim on the night of the murder awaiting questioning by you. 
Early reports tell us a few things. Firstly Matt proposed to his girlfriend and business partner Caitlin that night, she said yes. Caitlin and Matt had a big fight at some stage after the proposal. John was visibly upset when the proposal happened, and finally, Samantha, the victim's sister had recently found out that she would be receiving significantly less inheritance in their parents will.
