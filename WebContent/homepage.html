How to play

DeTalkive is a game of deduction. To play click on one of the active case pictures below. You'll be given the background of the crime and be presented with the suspects. Click on each suspect to talk with them and try to get the story straight!
The suspects won't always tell the truth, if something doesn't add up then call them out on it - tell them they're lying. If you catch the suspect lying they may become nervous and give more useful answers, catch them lying enough and they'll; give up their deceitful ways and become completely open, so be sure to ask them the same question more than once!
The best way to approach this is to ask all of the questions to all of the suspects to look for inconsistencies, then you can start accusing them of lying with confidence. Once you have the killer, tell them they're the murderer, or just openly ask them if they killed the suspect. You might be surprised with the answer. That's all there is to it! Click anywhere outside this box to get started.
